Swipe left to view the tutorial.

Create an assessment

You can easily create an assessment by clicking on the plus icon in the top-right corner in assessments tab, enter a location and click "save".

Complete an assessment

After creation, you will be taken to the sections page where you can choose the section and start answering the questions.

Each page contains one question and you will be able to choose from 1-5 or NA (non-applicable). You can click on the left and right arrows to navigate to the previous, respectively next question and save your answers.

View details/export/delete an assessment

You can view your answers, export them via email or delete the assessment by clicking on the buttons below each assessment.

Your profile

On the profile tab you will be able to view your details. When clicking on the settings icon in the top-right corner you will be able to view your settings: edit your details, review this tutorial or logout.
